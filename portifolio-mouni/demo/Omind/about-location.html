Timeline and Milestones

2005

o.mind Creatives makes a dynamic start in the field of the Supply chain and Logistics through a comprehensive communication system for the professionals of the sector: we publish the Supply Chain & Logistics magazine, we establish the online briefing through www.supply-chain.gr and we create a sectorial trade fair.

2011

We start dealing with the field of the food industry and more specifically the meat processing and charcuterie and we begin the publication of the new Meat Place magazine which was followed by the organization of the MEAT DAYS fair, a groundbreaking event for the Greek experience of the specific sector.

2013

We introduce new sections in the MEAT DAYS: dairy and cheesemaking industry (DAIRY EXPO) and frozen food (FROZEN FOOD).

2015

New publishing activities about mass catering and the new trends in the Greek gastronomy through issuing the Grill magazine, which had eagerly been awaited in the market. It was created to provide professionals in the field with knowledge, ideas and news. In addition, Dairy News, a magazine unique in its kind about dairy and cheesemaking products, is issued with highly targeted subject matter for the Greek professionals in cheesemaking and all the dairy industry which are in the vanguard of the Greek economy.

2016

The o.mind Creatives family has grown considerably in the previous ten years and now moves to new premises in Andrea Syngrou Avenue, a modern and state-of-the-art building in order for our new and greater needs to be accommodated.

2017

We bring out a unique publication, a contribution to the Greek Logistics market, entitled “DISCOVER GREEK LOGISTICS”, which travelled throughout Europe in order to promote the Greek entrepreneurship. This publication, which is exclusively in the English and German languages, contains a multi-page survey (funded by o.mind Creatives ) about the Greek logistics market, which was fervently embraced by the market. We continue together with you all to create new landmarks.
