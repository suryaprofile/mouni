Our Vision

o.mind Creatives’ vision constitutes both a personal and a corporate strategic target. Our mission has
always been the firm commitment to valid, full and objective briefing through our communication channels.
Through the years, we aspire to continue being the major communication pillar for professionals while our
trade fairs constitute a leading business meeting point where professionals will meet new people and
markets, acquire new ideas and make important deals for their companies.

Our Projects

o.mind Creatives is successfully involved in the field of trade fairs, publications and advertising. More specifically, our aim is to penetrate the markets in which we are
active in an organizational role and to create all the necessary preconditions for the effective communication among the involved parties, bringing together suppliers
